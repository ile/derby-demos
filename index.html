Toasts are short notifications messages that pop up somewhere on the screen at times.

Flash messages are short notifications messages that are a bit like toasts. However, there is an important difference; they are used when going to another url, while toasts are used on the same page and appear instantly. Flash messages appear only when the url is changed.
